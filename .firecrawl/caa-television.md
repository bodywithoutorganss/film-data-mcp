About CAA Television

CAA clients lead from both sides of the camera.

CAA represents successful creators, performers, personalities, and brands. Our clients work across the entire television landscape, from scripted programming to live sports broadcasts to reality series, on network and cable television and SVOD platforms.

**Literary**

We represent the showrunners, writers, directors, and producers creating the most exciting and award-winning programming on television.

**Scripted Television Talent**

CAA clients are among the most prolific and acclaimed actors and actresses in television. From established performers to emerging talent, our clients star in critically and commercially successful programming.

**Alternative & Nonscripted Television**

CAA clients create and produce reality programs, documentaries and docuseries, lifestyle television, and more.

**Alternative Television Talent**

CAA’s Alternative Television Talent include media personalities, hosts, and reality series stars.

**Sports & News Media Talent**

Our Sports Broadcasting group represents some of the world’s most prestigious talent, both in the booth and in the studio. CAA’s news division represents anchors, hosts, correspondents, and commentators.

**Intellectual Property Rights**

CAA represents the rights to many of the most compelling and successful books, magazines, podcasts, and other works produced into episodic television.
