# Where do I find contact information for an individual or company?

All contact information we have for a given individual or company is available on that person's or company's IMDbPro page.

#### Access to the information referenced below requires an IMDbPro Premium Subscription while members with an IMDbPro Basic Subscription may have limited access. Learn more on the [IMDbPro Subscription comparison page.](https://help.imdb.com/article/imdbpro/membership-benefits/which-imdbpro-subscription-is-right-for-me/GH39495X7KWY4PMP?ref_=helpms_helpart_inline)

### A Person's Contact Information

For example, let's look at [Natalie Portman's](https://pro-labs.imdb.com/name/nm0000204?ref_=helpms_ph_ic_findcontact) page. On this page, you'll find her employer and her reps (Talent Agents, Managers, Publicists, Legal reps, etc.) listed in the right column, or in the **Contacts** tab.

![Natalie Portman's contacts page](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/imdbpro/help/help_site_content/GXKMDZQ4GC752WBD/NPContacts.jpg)

* * *

### A Company's Contact Information

For example, the [United Talent Agency (UTA)](https://pro-labs.imdb.com/company/co0033208/?ref_=helpms_ph_ic_findcontact) page. On this page, you'll find office locations ("Branches"), staff, clients and filmography credits as well as other details.

**Branch**: Branch locations, along with other details will be listed in the **Contacts** section on the right.

**Staff**: You will see a partial list of staff in the middle with a link to view the entire staff roster on its own page. To see the full staff roster, click the **Staff** header, which will take you to the [Staff](https://pro-labs.imdb.com/company/co0033208/staff?ref_=helpms_ph_ic_findcontact) page. You can sort that page by name, title, STARmeter or branch to more easily find individuals.

**Clients**: You will see a partial list of clients below the staff box with a link to view the entire clients list on its own page.

To see the full client list, click the **Clients** header, which will take you to the [Clients](https://pro-labs.imdb.com/company/co0033208/clients?ref_=helpms_ph_ic_findcontact) page. The client list is sorted by STARmeter with each of the reps listed next to each client. If you are having trouble locating someone on the list, try using your browser's Find function by clicking _Ctrl + F_ and typing their name.

![UTA's company page](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/imdbpro/help/help_site_content/GXKMDZQ4GC752WBD/UTA.jpgG)
