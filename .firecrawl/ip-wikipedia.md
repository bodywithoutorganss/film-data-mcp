Impact Partners

**Impact Partners** is an American [film production](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Film_production "Film production") and [television production](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Television_production "Television production") company founded in 2007, by [Dan Cogan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dan_Cogan "Dan Cogan") and [Geralyn Dreyfous](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geralyn_Dreyfous "Geralyn Dreyfous"). The company primarily produces [documentary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Documentary "Documentary") films focusing on social issues.

They have produced such films as _[The Queen of Versailles](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Queen_of_Versailles "The Queen of Versailles")_ (2012), _[How to Survive a Plague](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/How_to_Survive_a_Plague "How to Survive a Plague")_ (2012), _[The Hunting Ground](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hunting_Ground "The Hunting Ground")_ (2015), _[Icarus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Icarus_(2017_film) "Icarus (2017 film)")_, which won the [Academy Award for Best Documentary Feature](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Academy_Award_for_Best_Documentary_Feature "Academy Award for Best Documentary Feature"), _[Of Fathers and Sons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Of_Fathers_and_Sons "Of Fathers and Sons")_ (2017), _[Won't You Be My Neighbor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Won%27t_You_Be_My_Neighbor%3F_(film) "Won't You Be My Neighbor? (film)")_ (2018), and _[On the Record](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/On_the_Record_(film) "On the Record (film)")_ (2020).

## History

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Impact_Partners&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: History")\]

In 2007, [Dan Cogan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dan_Cogan "Dan Cogan") and [Geralyn Dreyfous](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geralyn_Dreyfous "Geralyn Dreyfous") founded the company, focusing on financing and producing [documentary](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Documentary "Documentary") film and television projects, focusing on social issues.[\[1\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact_Partners#cite_note-1)

The company has produced films which have gone on to receive critical acclaim and awards, including _[Freeheld](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Freeheld_(2007_film) "Freeheld (2007 film)")_ (2007), which won [Academy Award for Best Documentary (Short Subject)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Academy_Award_for_Best_Documentary_(Short_Subject) "Academy Award for Best Documentary (Short Subject)"),[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact_Partners#cite_note-2) _[The Hunting Ground](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Hunting_Ground "The Hunting Ground")_ (2015), which was nominated for [Primetime Emmy Award for Exceptional Merit in Documentary Filmmaking](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Primetime_Emmy_Award_for_Exceptional_Merit_in_Documentary_Filmmaking "Primetime Emmy Award for Exceptional Merit in Documentary Filmmaking"),[\[3\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact_Partners#cite_note-3) _[Icarus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Icarus_(2017_film) "Icarus (2017 film)")_ (2017), which won the [Academy Award for Best Documentary Feature](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Academy_Award_for_Best_Documentary_Feature "Academy Award for Best Documentary Feature"),[\[4\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact_Partners#cite_note-4) _[Of Fathers and Sons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Of_Fathers_and_Sons "Of Fathers and Sons")_ (2017), which was nominated for Best Documentary Feature,[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact_Partners#cite_note-5) _[Divide and Conquer: The Story of Roger Ailes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Divide_and_Conquer:_The_Story_of_Roger_Ailes "Divide and Conquer: The Story of Roger Ailes")_ (2018), nominated for the Primetime Emmy Award for Exceptional Merit in Documentary Filmmaking,[\[6\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact_Partners#cite_note-6) and _The Apollo_ which won the Primetime Emmy for Exceptional Merit in Documentary Filmmaking.[\[7\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact_Partners#cite_note-7)

Apart from films, the company has produced television shows including _[Immigration Nation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Immigration_Nation "Immigration Nation")_ for [Netflix](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Netflix "Netflix"),[\[8\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact_Partners#cite_note-8) _[Allen v. Farrow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Allen_v._Farrow "Allen v. Farrow")_ for [HBO](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HBO "HBO"),[\[9\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact_Partners#cite_note-9) and _[The Lincoln Project](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Lincoln_Project_(TV_series) "The Lincoln Project (TV series)")_ for [Showtime](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Showtime_(TV_network) "Showtime (TV network)").[\[10\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact_Partners#cite_note-10)

In 2018, the company announced it would support the development of 4-8 non-fiction projects a year, ranging between $10k-100k.[\[11\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact_Partners#cite_note-11) In January 2020, [Jenny Raskin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jenny_Raskin "Jenny Raskin") was named executive director of the company.[\[12\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Impact_Partners#cite_note-12)
