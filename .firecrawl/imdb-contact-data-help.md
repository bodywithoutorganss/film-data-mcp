## Getting started

The term **Contact information** refers to the different types of connection between
companies and
people.

For a **Company ![](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/imdbpro/help/help_site_content/GWKFPSMXB9UGCSFW/twotone_business_black_18dp.png)**

- Direct contact _(email, fax, phone)_
- Employees
- Branch data _(address, contact info)_
- (If agency) Represented clients

For a **Person ![](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/imdbpro/help/help_site_content/GWKFPSMXB9UGCSFW/twotone_perm_identity_black_18dp.png)**

- Direct contact _(email, fax, phone)_
- Employer
- (If an agent) Any represented clients
- (If talent) Any representing agents

On the IMDbPro website, most contact information is located on the left hand side of a name or
company
page:

![](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/imdbpro/help/help_site_content/GWKFPSMXB9UGCSFW/verified_cntct.png)

However employees (for companies) and clients (for companies and agents) are listed under their
specific
tabs on the IMDbPro.com page.

## Guidelines

Contact information is only available on IMDbPro.com (and via the IMDbPro App), this means you
can only
view and
update this information with an IMDbPro membership.

information,
this means
we don't list historic contact information
that's no longer applicable.

For deceased individuals our policy is to create a new company for that person's
estate
(in the form of "Estate of John Doe") and allow it to have representation. For example, you may
choose
to list the Legal Representative or Publicist of an estate.

### Setting point agent (new!)

IMDbPro members can now control which of their representatives are designated as their point
contacts on IMDbPro - this will be clearly visible on their name page for other users to see.


The point agent will be always displayed at the top of all reps from the company.

**Things to keep in mind**:

- Only “ _talent_” reps can be chosen as point contacts - i.e. those
starting with “talent...” _(e.g. talent agent, talent agent-voice_
_etc.)_.

- The agent must be linked to a company in order to update your point agent.

- The company can have multiple point agents, but they must be different _types_ of
agent. For example, it's not possible to set two " _talent agent-voice_"
representatives as point contacts for the same company, but you can set a " _talent_
_agent-voice_" and " _talent_
_agent-theatrical_" point agent for that company.

- Following on from above, adding a new point will override any previous selection for the
same company and talent type.

#### Setting point contact when adding a new agent:

When adding a new talent agent to your page, the option to **Set as point**
**contact** will be shown at the bottom of the form - simply tick this and submit
your request for review.

![Animated image of setting point agent during addition.](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/imdbpro/help/help_site_content/GWKFPSMXB9UGCSFW/new_add_point.gif)

#### Updating an existing agents:

Using the pencil icon next to any contact information you can update talent agent
representation by selecting the company they're associated with, followed by the ![](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/imdbpro/help/help_site_content/GWKFPSMXB9UGCSFW/ellipses_trans.png) button and **Set as point**
**contact** \- you can also untick this option if you'd like to remove them as the
point agent.

![Animated image of setting point agent during correction.](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/imdbpro/help/help_site_content/GWKFPSMXB9UGCSFW/correct_agent_point.gif)

## Frequently asked questions

This section covers the most common questions we receive from customers just like you - hopefully the
answer to your
question is available below.

If not, you can reach out to a member of our support team via the [contact form](https://help.imdb.com/contact) and a
member of staff should be able to help you out further.

|     |
| --- |
| **My representative is right but their  contact details  are wrong?**<br>The contact information pulled through for an agent is done so from their<br>IMDbPro page.<br> <br>If the information is wrong on the agents page, this will be wrong on the<br>clients page<br>too.<br>To fix this, you'll need to submit an update directly to the agents page<br>to fix their<br>information - once approved, it'll update on any clients pages too. |

|     |
| --- |
| **My agent is wrong - can you update  it?**<br>We're not able to make changes on your behalf - you can submit the<br>update using the<br>instructions from the **How to**<br>**guide** section of this<br>article.<br>A member of the team will review and approve the update if eligible. |

|     |
| --- |
| **I no longer have IMDbPro, how do I  update incorrect  contact information?**<br>If you no longer have an IMDbPro membership and there's incorrect<br>information listed on<br>the page - you'll need to speak to somebody with an active membership<br>and ask them to<br>submit the update on your behalf.<br>If you don't know anybody else with an IMDbPro membership, please get in<br>touch using<br>out [contact\<br>form](https://help.imdb.com/contact) and a member of our team can remove the incorrect<br>information.<br>Please be aware that they'll not be able to put in new contact<br>information as this is a<br>feature of the IMDbPro membership. |

|     |
| --- |
| **How can I update my information if  the company and/or  agent isn't listed on IMDbPro?**<br>There are two ways to update your information if this happens - the best<br>way is to try<br>and submit the information via the ![](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/imdbpro/help/help_site_content/GWKFPSMXB9UGCSFW/editpage.png) or ![](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/imdbpro/help/help_site_content/GWKFPSMXB9UGCSFW/edit_your_page.png) buttons at the bottom of the<br>page. <br>Choose the option from the drop-down based on what you're trying to<br>achieve followed<br>by ![](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/imdbpro/help/help_site_content/GWKFPSMXB9UGCSFW/continue.png)<br>Once you've entered the company (or agent) information and hit ![](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/imdbpro/help/help_site_content/GWKFPSMXB9UGCSFW/check_updates.png) you'll be prompted to create<br>the company or<br>agent like below:<br>![](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/G/01/imdbpro/help/help_site_content/GWKFPSMXB9UGCSFW/add_new_comp2.png)<br>This may take some time for an editor to review and approve the<br>information.<br>The other option is only available if you're the page owner - in this<br>instance you can<br>update the information via your **[performer\**<br>**profile](https://help.imdb.com/article/imdbpro/manage-your-imdb-page/manage-your-performer-profile-credentials/GMLWQXHRJL84G66W?ref_=helpsrall#)**. |
